FESTIVAL FOR PEACE IN THE AFRICAN GREAT LAKES REGION 2022

"Playing for Change, Singing for Peace"

The Amani Festival is a non-profit organization whose objectives are: - to bring people together through culture and thus offer a festive space, far from everyday problems and the aftermath of war. - restore the image of Goma, eastern DRC and the Great Lakes region. The idea of ​​organizing a major music and dance festival was initiated by the trainers and management of the Goma Cultural Center with the aim of promoting peace, culture and peaceful coexistence in the region. Young volunteers, trainers from the cultural center of Goma all got together and decided to make culture one of the means of expression, awareness, promotion of peace.
